# KER Scoring Guide for Cyboquatic Pilots

KER = (Knowledge, EcoImpact, Risk-of-harm)

- **K (Knowledge)**: fraction of validated corridors, number of independent studies, and number of field pilots.
- **E (EcoImpact)**: combined benefit from deforestation reduction, pollutant removal, and sewer resilience.
- **R (Risk-of-harm)**: residual uncertainty and remaining eco-risk given current design and data.

`ecosafety-core::KerScore::from_inputs` computes KER from normalized inputs. All cyboquatic pilots must attach a KER score to their qpudatashards.
